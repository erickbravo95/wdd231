Overview

Purpose

The Guayaquil Chamber of Commerce has the following objectives: Promote the development of commerce, sources of wealth and business in general; seek the prosperity of its members, to whom it will provide support and cooperation for the development of their activities, and the necessary services within the legal provisions and this Statute; exercise a civic influence that results in the development of the city of Guayaquil and the achievement of its socio-economic and cultural objectives; as well as those from all over the country.
