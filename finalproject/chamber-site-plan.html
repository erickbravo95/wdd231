Overview

Purpose

Sabores Mexicanos aims to deliver an authentic experience of traditional Mexican cuisine, highlighting the cultural richness and unique flavors from different regions of Mexico. The website will provide information about the menu, promotions, special events, and the stories behind our dishes, creating a connection with food lovers and visitors interested in Mexican culture.

Scenarios

• What are the weekly specials and their prices? • Where is the restaurant located and how can I make an online reservation? • Are there any special events or promotions for Mexican Independence Day?
